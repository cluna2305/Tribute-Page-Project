Matthew Mercer

"How do you want to do this?"

Matthew Christopher Miller (born June 29, 1982), known professionally as Matthew Mercer, is an American voice actor, best known for his work in animations and video games; in addition to voice-over, he also developed the Dungeons & Dragons web series Critical Role, in which he plays the Dungeon Master.

Mercer began his career in high school doing English walla and additional characters in several Japanese anime and has since worked with a variety of media, including cartoons, video games and radio commercials. Mercer also produced the web series Fear News with the Last Girl and acts in several shows from Geek & Sundry and Nerdist.

Fun Facts

  • Matthew married Marisha Ray who is also a well know voice actor in 2017
  • Matthew is very well known for promoting charity programs in his work and has a page on his website dedicated to them, click here to learn more!
  • Matthew collaborated with Wizards of the coast to create a new book that has been officially introduced to the dungeons and dragon’s universe titled “Explorer’s Guide to Wildemount”. This was a monumental achievement to have one of Matthew’s passion projects be widely recognized in such a large scale.
